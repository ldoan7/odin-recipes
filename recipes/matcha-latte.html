Matcha Latte

Description

Iced matcha latte with non-dairy milk and honey

Ingredients

  • Matcha powder
  • Hot water
  • Milk of choice
  • Honey
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Mix matcha powder and hot water together.
  2. Add honey to taste.
  3. Pour milk over ice in a glass.
  4. Pour matcha mixture over milk.
