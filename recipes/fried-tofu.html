Fried Tofu

Description

Fried tofu with gochujang sauce

Ingredients

  • Tofu, firm and cubed
  • Corn starch
  • Gochujang
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Honey
  • Gochugaru

Steps

  1. Use a tofu press to get rid of as much water as possible from the tofu.
  2. Coat the tofu cubes in corn starch.
  3. Pan fry in vegetable oil.
  4. Mix the sauce ingredients in a separate bowl.
  5. Remove fried tofu from pan.
  6. Add sauce into the pan.
  7. Add the tofu back into the sauce and coat all sides.
